# How it Works: Sales Playbook Generator AI Worker

Creates customized sales strategies and pitch materials for each prospect This AI Worker ensures every prospect conversation is strategically informed and highly relevant. It analyzes the prospect's specific situation—current providers, investment challenges, organizational dynamics, recent events—and generates a customized playbook including talking points, competitive positioning, objection handling, and recommended next steps. The worker creates prospect-specific pitch materials, meeting agendas, and follow-up content, enabling even junior team members to conduct senior-level conversations.

## Triggers

- Meeting scheduled
- RFP received
- competitive situation identified
- prospect research request
- strategy session

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Situation Analysis Agent**
2. **Strategy Development Agent**
3. **Messaging Agent**

5. **Objection Preparation Agent**
6. **Agenda Creation Agent**
