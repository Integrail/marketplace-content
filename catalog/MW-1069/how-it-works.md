# How it Works: Competitive Differentiation AI Worker

This AI Worker arms every rep with deal-specific competitive intelligence. When a competitor enters a deal, it researches the specific competitive situation, generates personalized battlecards and comparison content, creates objection handling scripts, and produces prospect-ready differentiation materials. It ensures your team wins competitive deals with compelling, accurate positioning.

## Triggers

- Competitor identified in deal
- competitive objection received
- deal stage advancement

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Competitor Research Agent**
2. **Situation Analysis Agent**
3. **Battlecard Generation Agent**
4. **Objection Handling Agent**
5. **Content Assembly Agent**

## Knowledge Sources

- Competitive intelligence database
- win/loss analysis
- product comparison data
- customer feedback
- pricing intel

## Outputs

- Personalized battlecards
- competitive comparison charts
- objection handling scripts
- prospect-ready PDFs
