# How it Works: Business Case & Proposal AI Worker

This AI Worker produces executive-ready business cases and proposals that drive buying decisions. It synthesizes discovery information, builds ROI models using your value framework, creates compelling narrative documents, and generates professional proposals. It gives every rep the ability to deliver CFO-ready justification for every deal.

## Triggers

- Deal context
- discovery transcripts
- pricing inputs
- prospect financials
- specific requirements

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Discovery Analysis Agent**
2. **ROI Calculation Agent**
3. **Narrative Writing Agent**
4. **Proposal Assembly Agent**
5. **Design Agent**

## Knowledge Sources

- ROI calculation frameworks
- customer success data
- industry benchmarks
- pricing models
- proposal templates

## Outputs

- Complete business case document
- ROI model
- executive summary
- formal proposal
- pricing schedules
