# How it Works: Treasury Operations AI Worker

Manages bank relationships, intercompany funding, and debt/investment optimization This AI Worker elevates treasury from administrative task management to strategic capital optimization. It monitors all bank accounts for balance and activity, executes intercompany transfers to optimize entity-level liquidity, tracks covenant compliance across all debt instruments, and manages short-term investment positions. The worker handles bank fee analysis, relationship documentation, and ensures your capital structure supports business objectives while minimizing cost of capital.

## Triggers

- Daily bank reconciliation
- intercompany funding requests
- debt covenant calculations
- investment maturity
- bank RFP
- fee review

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Bank Monitoring Agent**
2. **Intercompany Netting Agent**
3. **Transfer Execution Agent**
4. **Covenant Tracking Agent**
5. **Investment Management Agent**
6. **Fee Analysis Agent**
7. **Documentation Agent**
