# How it Works: Social Media AI Worker

This AI Worker transforms your social media operations from a time-intensive bottleneck into a scalable content engine. It researches trending topics in your industry, writes platform-optimized posts for LinkedIn, X, Instagram, and Facebook, generates accompanying visuals, and schedules content directly to your social media management platform. It also coordinates employee activation campaigns by generating personalized content for team members to share, amplifying your reach without adding headcount.

## Triggers

- Content calendar
- campaign briefs
- product launches
- company news
- industry trends
- employee activation requests

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Research Agent**
2. **Writing Agent**
3. **Design Agent**
4. **Scheduling Agent**
5. **Activation Agent**

## Knowledge Sources

- Brand guidelines
- tone of voice documentation
- past high-performing posts
- competitor content
- industry news feeds
- product information

## Outputs

- Published social posts
- scheduled content queue
- employee activation content packages
- performance reports
