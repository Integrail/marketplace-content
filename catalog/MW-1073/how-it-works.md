# How it Works: Due Diligence & Analysis AI Worker

Conducts systematic due diligence across financial, operational, and market dimensions This AI Worker brings rigor and consistency to every due diligence process. It executes standardized analysis protocols across financial performance, operational metrics, market positioning, customer concentration, management quality, and legal/regulatory risks. The worker identifies anomalies, flags concerns, benchmarks against comparable transactions, and generates comprehensive diligence reports. Every deal receives thorough analysis regardless of team capacity constraints.

## Triggers

- Deal advancement to diligence
- VDR availability
- management meeting
- expert call
- workstream completion

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Workstream Coordination Agent**
2. **Financial Analysis Agent**
3. **Commercial Analysis Agent**
4. **Operations Analysis Agent**
5. **Legal/Regulatory Agent**
6. **Risk Synthesis Agent**
7. **Report Generation Agent**
