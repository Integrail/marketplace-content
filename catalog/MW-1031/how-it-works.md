# How it Works: Internal Candidate Sourcing AI Worker

Searches your ATS for qualified internal candidates and conducts personalized outreach This AI Worker mines your existing talent pool to find hidden gems. Given a role's requirements, it searches your ATS database for candidates who applied for similar positions in the past, scores them against current job criteria, and ranks the best matches. For each qualified candidate, it crafts personalized outreach explaining why they'd be a great fit and gauging their interestâ€” turning your ATS from a static database into an active sourcing engine.

## Triggers

- Job description
- qualification criteria
- years of experience
- location
- skills
- seniority level

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Job Analysis Agent**
2. **ATS Search Agent**
3. **Candidate Matching Agent**
4. **Scoring & Ranking Agent**
5. **Email Personalization Agent**
6. **Outreach Agent**

## Knowledge Sources

- Job requirements documentation
- candidate evaluation criteria
- internal mobility policies
- skills taxonomies
- role competency frameworks

## Outputs

- Ranked candidate list with match scores
- personalized outreach emails
- ATS status updates
- response tracking
