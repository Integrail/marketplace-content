# External Candidate Sourcing AI Worker — How It Works

This AI Worker handles the most challenging — and highest-value — recruiting work: converting
passive candidates into applicants.

## Agent Pipeline

1. **Search Execution Agent** — runs LinkedIn searches (Recruiter or standard) based on
   the provided search URL or saved candidate list.
2. **Profile Extraction Agent** — extracts comprehensive profile data for each prospect,
   including experience, skills, tenure, and career trajectory.
3. **Background Analysis Agent** — analyzes the candidate's background against job
   requirements to assess fit and identify specific talking points.
4. **Message Writing Agent** — crafts a highly personalized LinkedIn message and email
   for each prospect, referencing specific aspects of their experience.
5. **Approval Agent** — routes drafted messages to the recruiter for review before sending.
6. **LinkedIn Send Agent** — delivers approved messages via LinkedIn Recruiter or standard
   LinkedIn.
7. **Email Send Agent** — delivers the parallel email outreach to the candidate's verified
   address.

## What You Provide

- LinkedIn search URL or saved candidate list
- Role job description
- Outreach preferences (tone, focus areas, message length)

## What the Worker Delivers

- 50–100+ personalized touches per day per recruiter
- 3–5× improvement in response rate over template-based outreach
- 15+ hours/week saved on manual research and writing
- 40–60% increase in passive candidate pipeline
- Every message references candidate-specific details — zero generic templates
