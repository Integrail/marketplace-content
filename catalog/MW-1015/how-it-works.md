# How it Works: Scenario Planning AI Worker

Models business scenarios and provides decision support for strategic planning This AI Worker enables rapid what-if analysis that would take weeks manually. It builds scenario models based on key business drivers, quantifies the financial impact of strategic alternatives, and presents results in decision-ready formats. Whether evaluating M&A targets, assessing market entry options, or stress-testing for economic downturns, the worker delivers rigorous analysis that supports better strategic decisions. Scenario planning moves from annual exercises to continuous capability.

## Triggers

- Strategic planning cycle
- M&A evaluation
- market change
- risk assessment request
- board strategy session

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Scenario Definition Agent**
2. **Assumption Modeling Agent**
3. **Impact Calculation Agent**
4. **Sensitivity Analysis Agent**
5. **Comparison Agent**
6. **Visualization Agent**
7. **Recommendation Agent**

## Knowledge Sources

- Base financial models
- driver sensitivities
- market data
- competitive intelligence
- historical correlations
- risk factors
- valuation methodologies

## Outputs

- Scenario models
- sensitivity analyses
- comparison matrices
- probability-weighted outcomes
- executive presentations
- recommendation summaries
- risk assessments
