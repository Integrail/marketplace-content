# How it Works: SDR Team Lead AI Worker

This AI Worker operates as a tireless SDR manager, producing personalized outreach at scale. It researches each prospect and account, writes multi-touch email sequences personalized to their specific situation, creates follow- up cadences, and builds complete sequences directly in your sales engagement platform. Every rep gets personalized outreach for every prospect without the hours of manual research and writing.

## Triggers

- New leads assigned
- campaign launches
- account-based plays
- rep requests for personalized sequences

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Research Agent**
2. **Account Analysis Agent**
3. **Personalization Agent**
4. **Sequence Writing Agent**
5. **Build Agent**

## Knowledge Sources

- Prospect LinkedIn profiles
- company websites
- news articles
- CRM data
- past successful sequences
- competitor intel
- product value props

## Outputs

- Multi-touch email sequences
- personalization insights
- call scripts
- LinkedIn message templates
- built directly in platform
