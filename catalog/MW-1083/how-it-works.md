# How it Works: Employee Referral AI Worker

This AI Worker maximizes your highest-quality source of hire. It proactively notifies employees about roles matching their network, makes submitting referrals frictionless, and keeps referrers updated throughout the process. It matches employee networks against open roles, tracks referral bonuses, and ensures no referral falls through the cracks. Referred candidates are flagged and fast-tracked through your process.

## Triggers

- New requisition opened
- employee referral submission
- referral status change
- bonus eligibility milestone

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Matching Agent**
2. **Notification Agent**
3. **Submission Processing Agent**
4. **Status Update Agent**
5. **Bonus Tracking Agent**

## Knowledge Sources

- Open requisitions
- employee directory
- referral program rules
- bonus structure
- referral history

## Outputs

- Referral notifications to employees
- submission confirmations
- status updates
- bonus processing triggers
- program analytics
