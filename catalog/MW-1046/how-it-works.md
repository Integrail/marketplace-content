# How it Works: Lead Assessment AI Worker

Evaluates and scores prospects to prioritize highest-probability opportunities This AI Worker ensures your team focuses on prospects most likely to convert. It analyzes each lead against multiple dimensions—assets under management, investment philosophy alignment, current provider relationships, decision-making timeline, and propensity signals—to generate a comprehensive fit score. The worker identifies specific pain points, competitive vulnerabilities, and optimal entry strategies for each prospect, enabling personalized and highly effective outreach.

## Triggers

- New lead in CRM
- qualification request
- scoring model updates
- competitive intelligence
- deal stage advancement

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Data Collection Agent**
2. **Profile Analysis Agent**
3. **Fit Scoring Agent**
4. **Pain Point Identification Agent**
5. **Competition Analysis Agent**
6. **Recommendation Agent**
7. **CRM Update Agent**
