# How it Works: Learning Content & Certification AI Worker

Creates training materials, manages certification programs, and tracks completion This AI Worker transforms your L&D capacity from constrained to limitless. It creates training content on any topic—from compliance modules to skills development courses—in multiple formats. It manages certification programs including prerequisites, expiration tracking, and renewal reminders. For every employee, it maintains a complete learning record and proactively recommends relevant content based on role, career goals, and skill gaps. Your L&D team focuses on strategy while the AI handles production and administration.

## Triggers

- Content requests
- certification requirements
- completion tracking
- learning recommendations
- renewal alerts
