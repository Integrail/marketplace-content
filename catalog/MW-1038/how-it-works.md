# How it Works: Benefits Administration AI Worker

Handles benefits enrollment, changes, and questions throughout the employee lifecycle This AI Worker guides employees through the complexity of benefits decisions. During open enrollment, it explains plan options, compares coverage levels, and helps employees make informed choices. Year-round, it answers questions about coverage, processes life event changes, assists with claims questions, and ensures employees maximize their benefits. It transforms benefits administration from a seasonal HR burden into continuous, personalized employee support.

## Triggers

- Enrollment requests
- plan comparison questions
- life event changes
- coverage inquiries
- claims questions

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Question Classification Agent**
2. **Plan Comparison Agent**
3. **Enrollment Guidance Agent**
4. **Change Processing Agent**
5. **Carrier Coordination Agent**

## Knowledge Sources

- Benefits plan documents
- coverage summaries
- cost comparisons
- eligibility rules
- life event procedures
- carrier information

## Outputs

- Plan comparisons
- enrollment confirmations
- change processing
- coverage summaries
- claims guidance
- deadline reminders
