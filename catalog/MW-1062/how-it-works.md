# How it Works: Landing Page AI Worker

This AI Worker produces high-converting landing pages for every campaign. It writes persuasive headlines and copy based on proven conversion frameworks, designs page layouts optimized for lead capture, creates form configurations, and builds finished pages directly in your CMS or landing page platform. Whether you're gating a whitepaper, promoting a webinar, or driving demo requests, it delivers pages engineered for conversion.

## Triggers

- Campaign type
- offer description
- target audience
- desired CTA
- form fields required

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Strategy Agent**
2. **Headline Agent**
3. **Copywriting Agent**
4. **Design Agent**
5. **Form Configuration Agent**
6. **Build Agent**

## Knowledge Sources

- Conversion rate optimization best practices
- past landing page performance
- brand guidelines
- product messaging
- competitor pages

## Outputs

- Complete landing pages built in platform
- headline variants
- body copy
- form configurations
- thank you pages
