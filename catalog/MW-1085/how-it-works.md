# How it Works: Resume Screening AI Worker

This AI Worker processes every application instantly, evaluating candidates against role requirements and ranking them by fit. It eliminates the backlog of unreviewed resumes, ensures no qualified candidate is overlooked, and enables recruiters to focus on high-value activities. - successful hire profiles

## Outputs

- Ranked candidate lists
- match scores
- skills gap analysis
- top candidate alerts to recruiters
