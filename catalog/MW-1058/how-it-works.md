# How it Works: SEO Marketing AI Worker

This AI Worker delivers a complete SEO content operation. It analyzes your domain, identifies keyword opportunities based on search volume and competition, creates a prioritized content calendar, writes fully optimized blog posts with proper heading structure and internal linking, generates featured images, and publishes directly to your CMS. It monitors rankings and iteratively improves content based on performance data, creating a compounding organic traffic engine.

## Triggers

- Target keywords
- content themes
- product focus areas
- competitor URLs
- publishing schedule preferences
