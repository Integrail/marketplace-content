# How it Works: Candidate Communication AI Worker

This AI Worker ensures every candidate receives timely, personalized communication throughout your hiring process. It sends application confirmations, stage progression updates, interview prep materials, and status notifications automatically. It answers candidate questions via email, maintains your employer brand voice, and ensures no candidate is left wondering where they stand.

## Triggers

- Application received
- stage change
- interview scheduled
- candidate question received
- decision made

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Trigger Detection Agent**
2. **Message Personalization Agent**
3. **Timing Optimization Agent**
4. **Send Agent**
5. **Response Handling Agent**

## Knowledge Sources

- Email templates
- employer brand voice
- role details
- process stage requirements
- FAQ database

## Outputs

- Personalized candidate emails
- status updates
- interview prep packets
- FAQ responses
- rejection communications
