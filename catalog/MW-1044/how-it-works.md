# How it Works: Employee Engagement AI Worker

Monitors sentiment, analyzes feedback, and identifies retention risks This AI Worker gives you early warning on engagement issues before they become resignations. It conducts pulse surveys at optimal intervals, analyzes open-ended feedback for themes and sentiment, and identifies employees or teams showing warning signs. Beyond surveys, it monitors signals like goal progress, recognition frequency, and collaboration patterns to build a complete engagement picture. HR and managers get actionable insights to intervene proactively.

## Triggers

- Pulse survey deployment
- sentiment analysis requests
- flight risk alerts
- engagement reporting
- intervention tracking

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Survey Deployment Agent**
2. **Response Analysis Agent**
3. **Sentiment Classification Agent**
4. **Risk Scoring Agent**
5. **Alert & Recommendation Agent**
