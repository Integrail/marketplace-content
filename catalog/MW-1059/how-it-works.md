# How it Works: Video Marketing AI Worker

This AI Worker transforms video content from expensive, time-consuming productions into scalable content streams. It writes video scripts, generates AI-powered videos with synthetic presenters or animated explainers, creates thumbnails and titles optimized for engagement, and prepares videos for distribution across platforms. Whether you need product explainers, thought leadership content, or social clips, it delivers professional video content without cameras, studios, or editing suites.

## Triggers

- Video topics
- key messages
- target length
- style preferences (presenter, animated, screen capture)
- distribution channels

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Script Writing Agent**
2. **Storyboard Agent**
3. **Video Generation Agent**
4. **Editing Agent**
5. **Thumbnail Agent**
6. **Distribution Agent**

## Knowledge Sources

- Brand guidelines
- product information
- past video performance
- competitor videos
- audience preferences
- script templates

## Outputs

- Full-length videos
- short-form clips
- thumbnails
- titles and descriptions
- distribution schedule
