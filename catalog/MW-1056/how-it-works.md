# How it Works: Content Marketing AI Worker

This AI Worker operates as your always-on content team, producing long-form thought leadership assets that would typically require weeks of work. It conducts deep research on topics you define, synthesizes insights into compelling narratives, writes complete guides and whitepapers, designs professional layouts, and prepares assets for distribution. From a single brief, it delivers publication-ready content that positions your brand as an industry authority.

## Triggers

- Content briefs
- topic requests
- campaign themes
- product launches
- quarterly content plans

1. **Research Agent**
2. **Outline Agent**
3. **Writing Agent**
4. **Editing Agent**
5. **Design Agent**
6. **QA Agent**
