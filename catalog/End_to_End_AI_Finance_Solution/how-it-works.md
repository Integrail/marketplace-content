# End-to-End AI Finance Solution — How It Works

15 specialized AI Workers cover every stage of your finance function across three suites.

## Suite 1 — Cash Operations

Optimizes working capital from invoice receipt through payment execution.

### Accounts Payable (AP) AI Worker
Ingests invoices from any source (email, portal, EDI, paper), extracts data with high accuracy,
matches against POs and receiving documents, routes exceptions intelligently, and queues
approved invoices for optimal payment timing.
- 78% touchless processing rate
- Invoice processing time reduced from 12 days to 2 days
- Cost per invoice reduced from $15 to $2.40

### Order to Cash (O2C) AI Worker
Generates accurate invoices from sales orders, delivers through customer-preferred channels,
monitors payment status, executes intelligent dunning, and applies cash automatically.
- DSO reduced by 12 days on average
- 97% cash application accuracy on first pass
- Dispute resolution reduced from 18 days to 4 days

### Cash Flow Management AI Worker
Aggregates balances across all bank accounts and entities, forecasts inflows and outflows,
and recommends actions to optimize liquidity.
- 94% forecast accuracy at 30-day horizon
- Real-time position across 100% of accounts
- 23% reduction in borrowing costs

### Treasury Operations AI Worker
Monitors all bank accounts, executes intercompany transfers, tracks covenant compliance,
and manages short-term investment positions.
- Intercompany settlement reduced from 5 days to same-day
- 100% automated covenant monitoring with 30-day early warning
- $2.4M annual interest expense savings (mid-market example)

### Payment Processing AI Worker
Validates payment details against master data, detects fraud anomalies, executes payments
through optimal channels (ACH, wire, check, virtual card), and maintains complete audit trails.
- 99.9% of fraudulent payments blocked
- Payment processing time reduced from 4 hours to 15 minutes per batch
- Payment error rate reduced from 2.3% to 0.1%

- 85% of recurring entries processed without intervention
- 100% of accounts reconciled within 24 hours of close
- 60% reduction in pre-close reconciliation effort

### Financial Close AI Worker
Maintains the close calendar, assigns tasks to owners, tracks completion in real-time, manages
dependencies, and escalates bottlenecks automatically.
- Close cycle time reduced from 10 days to 4 days
- 98% of tasks completed on schedule
- 45% reduction in close-period overtime

Collects trial balances from all entities, maps charts of accounts, performs currency translation,
generates intercompany eliminations, and produces fully consolidated statements.
- Consolidation reduced from 3 days to 4 hours
- 99.8% accuracy on intercompany eliminations
- Handles 100+ entities without additional effort

### Compliance & Audit AI Worker

before auditors do, and responds to PBC requests instantly.
- 100% of key controls monitored continuously
- Audit request turnaround reduced from 3 days to 2 hours
- 22% reduction in external audit fees

### Regulatory Reporting AI Worker
Tracks all regulatory requirements across jurisdictions, generates filings in required formats,
validates against regulatory rules, and submits through appropriate channels.
- 100% on-time filing rate
- Zero penalties from late or incorrect filings
- Automated filings across 50+ jurisdictions
