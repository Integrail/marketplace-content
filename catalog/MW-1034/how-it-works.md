# How it Works: Interview Preparation AI Worker

Generates customized interview questions and coordinates candidate scheduling This AI Worker bridges the gap between qualification and conversation. For Strong Match candidates, it generates validation questions that probe depth of expertise. For Potential Fit candidates, it creates questions that assess whether gaps are bridgeable. It then accesses recruiter calendars, sends professional scheduling invitations to candidates, and delivers comprehensive interview prep packages—including candidate profiles and customized question sets—so recruiters walk into every call fully prepared.

## Triggers

- Qualified candidate list
- recruiter availability
- interview preferences
- screening priorities

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Candidate Classification Agent**
2. **Question Generation Agent**
3. **Calendar Integration Agent**
4. **Email Invitation Agent**
5. **Prep Package Agent**
6. **Confirmation Agent**

## Knowledge Sources

- Interview question frameworks
- screening best practices
- candidate assessment methodologies
- role competency models
- recruiter preferences

## Outputs

- Customized interview questions per candidate
- scheduling invitations
- recruiter prep packages (PDF)
- calendar events
- confirmation tracking
