# How it Works: Interview Debrief AI Worker

This AI Worker ensures structured, timely interview feedback. It prompts interviewers immediately after meetings, collects standardized scorecard data, synthesizes feedback across the interview panel, and surfaces hiring recommendations. It identifies consensus and disagreement, flags potential bias patterns, and gives hiring managers the complete picture to make faster, better decisions.

## Triggers

- Interview completed
- scorecard submitted
- all feedback received
- hiring manager review request

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Interview Detection Agent**
2. **Feedback Collection Agent**
3. **Synthesis Agent**
4. **Analysis Agent**
5. **Recommendation Agent**

## Knowledge Sources

- Scorecard templates
- competency frameworks
- role requirements
- interviewer history
- calibration data

## Outputs

- Scorecard collection prompts
- synthesized feedback reports
- panel consensus analysis
- hiring recommendations
