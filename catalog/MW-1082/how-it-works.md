# How it Works: Recruitment Marketing AI Worker

This AI Worker powers your employer brand presence. It creates career page content, employee spotlight stories, "day in the life" content, and social media posts that attract candidates to your brand. It develops recruitment marketing campaigns for hard-to-fill roles and high-volume hiring initiatives. All content is optimized for the channels where your target candidates spend time.

## Triggers

- Campaign brief
- hiring surge notification
- new office/team launch
- employee story submission

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Strategy Agent**
2. **Content Creation Agent**
3. **Design Agent**
4. **Channel Optimization Agent**
5. **Publishing Agent**

## Knowledge Sources

- Employer brand guidelines
- EVP messaging
- employee testimonials
- company culture documentation
- competitor employer brands

## Outputs

- Career page content
- social posts
- recruitment campaigns
- employee spotlight content
- campaign performance reports
