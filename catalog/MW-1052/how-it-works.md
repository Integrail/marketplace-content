# How it Works: Onboarding Workflow AI Worker

This AI Worker ensures every new hire has a seamless Day One experience. It coordinates across HR, IT, facilities, and hiring managers to ensure equipment is ordered, accounts are provisioned, workspace is ready, and orientation is scheduled. It tracks completion of all onboarding tasks, sends reminders to responsible parties, and ensures nothing falls through the cracks.

## Triggers

- Offer accepted
- start date confirmed
- task deadline approaching
- task completed

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Workflow Initiation Agent**
2. **Task Assignment Agent**
3. **Tracking Agent**
4. **Reminder Agent**
5. **Escalation Agent**

## Knowledge Sources

- Onboarding checklists by role/location
- equipment standards
- system access requirements
- orientation schedules

## Outputs

- Onboarding workflow initiation
- task assignments
- status dashboards
- reminder notifications
- escalation alerts
