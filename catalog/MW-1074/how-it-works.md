# How it Works: SAM Matching AI Worker

Connects buyers with sellers by matching investment criteria with opportunities This AI Worker creates value by making markets more efficient. It maintains comprehensive profiles of buyer preferences and seller offerings, then identifies high-probability matches based on strategic fit, financial parameters, and timing alignment. The worker facilitates introductions, provides match rationale to both parties, and tracks progression through the deal process. Whether you're advising sellers or representing buyers, the worker accelerates successful transactions.

## Triggers

- New buy-side mandate
- new sell-side engagement
- criteria update
- match request
- market sounding

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Profile Ingestion Agent**
2. **Criteria Matching Agent**
3. **Fit Scoring Agent**
4. **Conflict Check Agent**
5. **Introduction Facilitation Agent**
6. **Progress Tracking Agent**
