# How it Works: Order to Cash (O2C) AI Worker

Accelerates revenue collection from invoice generation through cash application This AI Worker compresses your order-to-cash cycle and improves collection rates. It generates accurate invoices from sales orders and contracts, delivers them through customer-preferred channels, monitors payment status, executes intelligent dunning sequences, and applies cash automatically when payments arrive. For disputes, it gathers documentation, routes to the right resolver, and tracks through resolution. Every action optimizes for faster collection while preserving customer relationships.

## Triggers

- Sales order completion
- contract milestones
- payment due dates
- remittance receipt
- dispute notification
- customer inquiry

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Invoice Generation Agent**
2. **Delivery Agent**
3. **Payment Monitoring Agent**
4. **Dunning Sequence Agent**
5. **Cash Application Agent**
6. **Dispute Management Agent**
7. **Credit Review Agent**
