# How it Works: SAM Discovery AI Worker

Maps your Serviceable Addressable Market to identify growth opportunities This AI Worker gives you a complete picture of your market opportunity. It identifies every potential client in your target segments, maps competitive relationships, surfaces whitespace opportunities, and tracks market share dynamics. The worker continuously updates as new entrants emerge, existing players evolve, and market conditions shift. Your business development team operates from a living market map rather than static lists, enabling strategic territory planning and resource allocation.

## Triggers

- Target market definitions
- segmentation criteria
- competitive set
- geographic scope
- AUM thresholds
- strategy focus areas

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Market Scanning Agent**
2. **Entity Identification Agent**
3. **Segmentation Agent**
4. **Competitive Mapping Agent**
5. **Opportunity Scoring Agent**
6. **Visualization Agent**
7. **Update Monitoring Agent**
