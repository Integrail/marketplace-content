# How it Works: Offer Generation AI Worker

This AI Worker eliminates the bottleneck between verbal offer and written letter. It pulls approved compensation data, applies the correct offer template, personalizes terms based on the candidate and role, and generates a complete offer package for recruiter review. Legal and compliance language is automatically included. Once approved, it sends the offer and tracks acceptance.

## Triggers

- Hiring decision made
- compensation approved
- offer request from recruiter

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Data Collection Agent**
2. **Template Selection Agent**
3. **Personalization Agent**
4. **Compliance Check Agent**
5. **Delivery Agent**

## Knowledge Sources

- Compensation bands
- offer templates
- benefits summaries
- legal language library
- equity guidelines

## Outputs

- Personalized offer letters
- compensation summaries
- e-signature requests
- acceptance tracking
- decline follow-up
