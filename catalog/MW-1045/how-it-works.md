# How it Works: Lead Generation AI Worker

Identifies potential clients through market signals and trigger events continuously This AI Worker ensures your pipeline never runs dry. It monitors thousands of signals—leadership changes, fundraising announcements, M&A activity, regulatory filings, portfolio company exits, succession planning triggers—and identifies prospects who match your ideal client profile at the moment they're most likely to need your services. The worker aggregates data from public filings, news sources, social media, and industry databases to surface opportunities before your competitors see them.

## Triggers

- Ideal client profile criteria
- target market segments
- trigger event definitions
- exclusion lists
- geographic focus
