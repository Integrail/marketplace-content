# How it Works: Email Marketing AI Worker

This AI Worker automates your entire email marketing production workflow. It writes subject lines optimized for open rates, crafts compelling email copy tailored to each audience segment, designs responsive email templates, and builds finished emails directly in your marketing automation platform. From newsletters to nurture sequences to promotional campaigns, it produces professional email assets ready to send.

## Triggers

- Campaign brief
- audience segment
- offer/CTA
- send date
- email type (newsletter, nurture, promotional)

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Strategy Agent**
2. **Subject Line Agent**
3. **Copywriting Agent**
4. **Design Agent**
5. **Build Agent**
6. **QA Agent**

## Knowledge Sources

- Past email performance
- subject line testing data
- brand guidelines
- product information
- audience preferences
- competitor emails

## Outputs

- Complete email campaigns built in platform
- subject line variants
- preview text
- email copy
- designed templates
