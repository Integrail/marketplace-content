# How it Works: Lead/Account Scoring & Enrichment AI Worker

This AI Worker transforms your CRM from a static database into an intelligent revenue engine. It researches every contact and account in your database, enriches records with firmographic, technographic, and intent data, applies predictive scoring models to identify who's most likely to buy, and segments your database for targeted campaign activation. It surfaces the accounts and contacts that deserve immediate attention.

## Triggers

- Scheduled enrichment cycles
- new lead ingestion
- campaign planning requests
- sales prioritization requests

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Data Collection Agent**
2. **Research Agent**
3. **Enrichment Agent**
4. **Scoring Agent**
5. **Segmentation Agent**

## Knowledge Sources

- CRM data
- enrichment APIs
- intent data providers
- website visitor data
- product fit criteria
- ideal customer profile

## Outputs

- Enriched CRM records
- lead/account scores
- prioritized lists
- campaign segments
- account insights
