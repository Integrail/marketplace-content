# How it Works: Applicant Qualification & Ranking AI Worker

Screens all incoming applicants against job requirements and ranks by fit This AI Worker ensures no qualified candidate slips through the cracksâ€”and no unqualified candidate wastes recruiter time. For every new applicant, it parses their resume, extracts key qualifications, matches them against job requirements, detects red flags, and applies a weighted scoring algorithm. Candidates are categorized as Strong Match, Potential Fit, Weak Match, or Unclear, with all results automatically updated in your ATS. Recruiters start each day with a prioritized queue instead of an overwhelming inbox.

## Triggers

- New applicants in ATS (automated trigger)
- role requirements
- screening criteria
- scoring weights

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Applicant Retrieval Agent**

3. **Requirements Matching Agent**
4. **Red Flag Detection Agent**
5. **Scoring Agent**
6. **ATS Update Agent**
