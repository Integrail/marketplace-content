# How it Works: Advertising AI Worker

This AI Worker generates complete advertising campaigns across every paid channel. It writes compelling ad copy with multiple variants for A/B testing, designs eye-catching visuals sized for each platform, creates audience targeting recommendations, and organizes assets for easy campaign deployment. From LinkedIn Sponsored Content to Google Search Ads to Meta campaigns, it produces the creative assets that fuel your paid acquisition engine.

## Triggers

- Campaign brief
- target audience
- offer/CTA
- budget parameters
- channel priorities

## Agent Orchestration

1. **Strategy Agent**
2. **Copywriting Agent**
3. **Design Agent**
4. **Variant Generation Agent**
5. **Platform Sizing Agent**

## Knowledge Sources

- Past ad performance data
- competitor ads
- brand guidelines
- product value props
- audience insights
- platform best practices

## Outputs

- Ad copy variants
- display ads
- video ads
- carousel ads
- platform-specific assets
- A/B testing matrix
